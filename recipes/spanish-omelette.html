Spanish Omelette

Description

The Spanish Omelette is a simple dish of eggs and some vegetables, usually onions, tomatoes, garlic and coriander. Salt and black pepper are the main seasonings used.

This meal is usually served for breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 Tomato
  • 1 Clove of garlic
  • 2 Tea spoons of vegetable oil
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Coriander

Steps

  1. In a container, whisk the eggs
  2. Cut up the onion, tomato and garlic. Do not mix!
  3. In a shallow frying pan, heat up the oil
  4. Once the oil is hot, add the onion and garlic
  5. When the onion turns golden brown, add the tomato while stirring
  6. Pour in the egg
  7. Season with salt and black pepper
  8. Once the eggs are cooked, plate and garnish with coriander
