Githeri

Description

Githeri is a meal made of primarily maize (corn) and beans. These are first boiled until cooked, then fried with onions and tomatoes. Other ingredients can be added during frying such us garlic, ginger, bell peppers or any other appropriate spice.

Githeri can be served accompanied by rice, chapati or just by itself

Ingredients

  • 1/4kg of Maize
  • 1/4kg of Beans
    • <1i>One large Onion
  • Two medium sized Tomatoes
  • Vegetable Cooking Oil

Steps

  1. Boil the maize and beans in water until cooked
  2. Cut up the onion and tomatoes into small pieces. Do not mix!
  3. In a sufuria heat some cooking oil - 2 tbl spoons - until hot
  4. Fry the onions until golden brown.
  5. Add the tomatoes to the onions while stirring and cook until there is a paste.
  6. Add the githeri and stir until everything is well mixed up
  7. Cook for ten minutes
  8. Serve with an accompaniment of your choice
